## Long description

> Toowoomba Roof Repairs is a QBCC-licensed, AEPMA-member termite inspection and treatment contractor servicing the full Toowoomba Region LGA — Glenvale, Wilsonton, Highfields, Rangeville, Centenary Heights, Mermaid Beach, Burleigh Heads, Palm Beach, Coolangatta, canal estates (Sanctuary Cove, Sovereign Islands, Hope Island, Paradise Point) and hinterland (Mudgeeraba, Tallai, Tamborine).
>
> Five service lines: AS 4349.3 pre-purchase termite inspection ($450-$650, written report for settlement); AS 3660.2 annual inspection ($250-$450, mandatory yearly compliance check); chemical soil treatment ($2,500-$5,500, Termidor SC / Premise 200SC non-repellent perimeter injection, 8-10 year barrier); baiting systems ($1,800-$3,500 install + $400/yr monitoring, Sentricon Always Active / Exterra / Trelona ATBS in-ground stations); post-construction protection on new builds ($1,800-$3,500, AS 3660.1 reticulation + perimeter for Form 17 / Form 21 compliance).
>
> The Toowoomba is one of Australia's highest natural termite-pressure zones. Coptotermes acinaciformis — the most destructive Australian termite species — is at peak activity in coastal QLD year-round, with no seasonal slowdown. Coupled with the LGA's mix of pre-1995 beachside cottage stock (expired chemical barriers), 1995-2008 reticulation-system stock (often expired without renewal), canal-estate elevated water tables, and bushland-interface acreage with high natural colony density, the inspection methodology and treatment specification matters more here than in most of Australia.
>
> Every inspection includes a calibrated moisture meter, thermal imaging camera (for stud-wall colony detection), borescope (for inaccessible cavity inspection), termite acoustic detector (for active colony confirmation) and foundation probe. Reports follow AS 3660.2 or AS 4349.3 format with 20-50 photos, moisture-reading log by location, suspect-area identification, chemical-barrier status assessment, treatment recommendations with cost estimates. Settlement-grade documentation suitable for conveyancers and insurance records.
>
> Same/next-day inspections most weeks. 24-hour turnaround on written reports. Termite Treatment Certificate filed under the meter box on every treatment job. Annual / 6-monthly inspection schedule available for high-risk properties.
>
> QBCC pest-management licensed. AEPMA (Australian Environmental Pest Managers Association) member. Termite Inspector Certified. ABN 91 664 546 061.
>
> Call (03) 9003 0108 Monday to Saturday 7am–5pm AEST, or visit toowoombaroofrepairs.com.au.
